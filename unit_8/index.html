Bee Population

Did you know that annually beekeepers report an average loss of 45.1% of bee hives and 31.1% of commercial hives?

Bees play a key role in the international agriculture. Bees are irreplaceable pollinators, about a third of our food including apples, nuts, summer fruits, alfalfa, and guar bean rely on bees. In the U.S. alone this tranlsates to $15 billion in agriculture production.

A recent study conducted by EPA-USDA concludes that the Honeybee Colony Collapse Disorder (CCD) occurs due to an amalgam of factors ranging from cell phone towers and increased use of pesticides especially in the US to shrinking habitats, multiple viruses, and poor nutrition and genetics. However, the single biggest factors attributed to the Colony Collapse Disorder (CCD) is the Arroa Destructor, a type of mite found to be highly resistant to the insecticides.
